Negative Scenario:

If Browser Crashes / Quizzer closes the Browser by mistake during the quiz


Scenario 1: The quizzer still has time. Duration of quiz 20 Mins , Quiz was started 11 minutes back, 9 minutes left. Quizzer logs back in , he should be able to access the quiz with all the answers that he had saved before.


Scenario 2: The quizzer does not have time Left. Duration of the quiz 20 Mins , Quiz was started 25 mins back. Display message that the quiz attempt is over.
